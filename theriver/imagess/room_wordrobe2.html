Located in Lahore, 24.1 km from Wagah Border, Hotel Pak Heritage provides accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel features family rooms.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the hotel.

Alhamra Art Center is a 17-minute walk from Hotel Pak Heritage, while Bagh-e-Jinnah is 2.1 km away. The nearest airport is Allama Iqbal International Airport, 14.5 km from the accommodation.

Hotel Pak Heritage has been welcoming Booking.com guests since Feb 10, 2016
